How to make: Bread

Description:

a Filipino dish made from pork jowl and ears (maskara), pork belly, and chicken liver which is usually seasoned with calamansi, onions, and chili peppers. It is very delicious

  • Pork
  • Lime
  • Eggs
  • Soy Sauce

Steps:

  1. Marinate Meat
  2. Cook Meat
  3. Mix in Soy Sauce
  4. Add water
  5. Put egg
