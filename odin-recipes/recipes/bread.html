How to make: Bread

Description:

bread, baked food product made of flour or meal that is moistened, kneaded, and sometimes fermented. A major food since prehistoric times, it has been made in various forms using a variety of ingredients and methods throughout the world. It is very delicious

Ingredients:

  • Yeast
  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Water

Steps:

  1. Mix dry ingredients
  2. Blend
  3. Simultaneously add yolk
  4. Add water
  5. Put in oven
